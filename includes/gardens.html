Heartland Gardens is a community developed in the foothillls of Gilroy, California. It lays adajacent to to the Uvas Creek Presevre. They are expanding the development and had to tie into an existing reclaimed water line.

The new line is 3,500' of 30" X 50' DR11, each section was fused in increments of 450' with flanged ends to make tieing into the line easier. A MegaMc 1648 was used for the fusing process.
